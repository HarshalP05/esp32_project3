Smart Home Application

A smart home with a doorbell camera, temperature and humidity sensor, and gas sensor enhances security, comfort, and safety. The doorbell camera offers remote viewing of visitors, the temperature and humidity sensor adjusts climate control for comfort and efficiency, and the gas sensor alerts to harmful gases, ensuring a safe living environment.
